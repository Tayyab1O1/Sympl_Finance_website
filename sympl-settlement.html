FAQ

The questions that usually come up before the first review.

01Can you support IRCC contract reporting?

Yes. We build the books around contract budgets, claim periods, variance notes, and funder categories so reporting is prepared throughout the month, not rebuilt at deadline.

02How do you handle staff funded by multiple contracts?

We set up a salary allocation model based on your program delivery and apply it with each payroll run. You get an audit-ready schedule showing exactly how costs were charged.

03Can reports match funder templates?

Yes. We map your chart of accounts to funder categories so the same set of books can produce clean internal, board, and funder-ready views.

04Do you work with organizations that have several programs?

Yes. Multi-program nonprofits are where this structure helps most. We separate contracts, shared costs, payroll allocations, and reporting timelines while keeping one clean accounting file.
