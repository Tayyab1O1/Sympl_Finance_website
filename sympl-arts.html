FAQ

The questions that usually come up before the first review.

01Can you handle artist and contractor payments?

Yes. We classify every guest artist, performer, and casual hire correctly as T4 or T4A, apply the right union rates, and keep records ready for year-end T4A issuance, with the exact process refined to fit how your organization already works.

02Can you reconcile ticketing, rentals, memberships, and donations?

Yes. We map each revenue source into the books, reconcile monthly, and keep earned revenue separate where your reporting or board package needs that view.

03Can you help with CADAC reporting?

Yes. We keep your books structured to match CADAC's categories throughout the year, so when reporting season comes around, the numbers are ready to pull, no scrambling through spreadsheets to reverse-engineer the format.

04Do we need to leave QuickBooks Online?

No. Your books stay in your own QuickBooks Online account. We work inside it, clean up the structure where needed, and keep you in full visibility.
