Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Sympl Finance ("Sympl", "we", "us") is part of Sympl Solutions and provides bookkeeping, payroll, reporting, and finance leadership services to nonprofit organizations. This policy explains how we handle personal information collected through this website, in line with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

What we collect

We only collect personal information that you choose to give us through the forms on this site:

  • Consultation requests — your name, organization, work email, and anything you tell us about your situation.
  • Quote requests — your name, organization details (type, budget range, staff count, software), work email, phone number, and notes.
  • Resource downloads — your email address.

We do not run advertising trackers and we do not sell personal information to anyone.

How we use it

  • To respond to your inquiry and prepare the consultation or quote you asked for.
  • To send you the resource you requested.
  • To keep basic records of who has contacted us.

We only use your information for the purpose you gave it to us, and we ask for your consent before using it for anything else.

How form submissions are delivered

When you submit a form, its contents are relayed to our team's email inboxes by FormSubmit, a form-delivery service. The information is transmitted over an encrypted connection and is not stored by us anywhere other than our email system afterward.

Other services this site uses

  • Hosting — this website is hosted on Vercel, which may log standard technical data (such as IP addresses) needed to serve pages securely.
  • Fonts — pages load typefaces from Google Fonts, which involves your browser requesting font files from Google's servers.

Retention

We keep inquiry emails for as long as needed to serve you or as required for our professional record-keeping obligations, and we delete them when they are no longer needed.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to show you the personal information we hold about you, correct it, or delete it. You can also withdraw consent for us to contact you. To do any of these, email us at contact@symplsolutions.com and we'll respond within a reasonable time.

Questions

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or how your information is handled, contact us at contact@symplsolutions.com. If you're not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.

This policy covers this website only. Client engagements are governed by the confidentiality and data-handling terms in your service agreement.
